HowWeWork

01

Preparation

From your general requirement we will shape them up into some development scope and proposed them back as a solution to you. We can discuss and decide which scope that you want to develop.

02

Drafting

Based on the scope we will create more details layout and content within wireframes. After we got approval from your side about the wireframes then we will create a good UI/UX design that is suitable to your business and align to your company brand guidelines.

03

Programming

In parallel after the wireframes got approved we will start back-end programming based on the platform required. On the other hand we will start front-end programming after we got the design approved by you. We implement best practice programming and security standard for every project.

04

Testing and Delivery

While doing the programming, we have a dedicated QA to check the functionality and quality of our work to make sure that we always deliver the best result for you. After we have confirmed internally then we will demonstrate and share the progress to you. If all requirements has been developed based on the proposed scope than we will deliver the project to you.
